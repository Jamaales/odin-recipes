Ingredients

  • 12 oz whole-wheat lasagna noodles
  • 8 oz lean ground beef
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 8 oz portobello mushrooms, diced

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees
  2. Cook the noodles according to directions on the package
  3. Heat large nonstick skillet over a medium high heat
  4. Add the oil to the same pan and heat over medium heat
  5. Spread 1 cup of tomato sauce on the bottom
